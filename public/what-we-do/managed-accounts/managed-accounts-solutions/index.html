Separately Managed Accounts (SMA)

Praemium’s market-leading SMA provides financial advisers and their clients with a broad choice of domestic and international model portfolios, professionally managed to a defined investment strategy. The SMA includes:

  • Superannuation/pension, SMSF and investment and savings solutions.
  • Access to > 350 professionally managed model portfolios and 80 Investment Managers with a world class rebalancing engine to ensure investors are always in-line with the chosen strategy.
  • Digital, 24/7 adviser and client reporting of investment holdings, corporate actions, performance and tax positions.
  • Value for money for investors and transformational efficiencies for advice businesses.

Less pen pushing, more wealth creating.

Praemium provides the broadest range of SMA model portfolios and underlying investments, including domestic and international listed securities, Exchange Traded Products (ETPs), unlisted managed funds and cash, so you can create a truly diversified portfolio for your clients.

These model portfolios are actively managed by Investment Managers and rebalanced automatically by Praemium's world-class rebalancing engine and integrated Managed Accounts platform.

Investors should consult their financial adviser and the relevant SMA product disclosure documents before considering investment in any financial product offered by Praemium.

The Praemium SMA features:

  • Low minimum investment and value for money at all account sizes.
  • Competitive admin fees and low brokerage through aggregated trading and Praemium's FUM scale.
  • Beneficial ownership of investments.
  • The industry benchmark in corporate actions, performance and tax reporting.
  • Customisations via substitutions, exclusions or locking of investment holdings.
  • Digital acceptance of applications and in-specie transfer of assets.
  • A truly innovative digital Investor Portal available via any wifi or internet enabled device.
  • A competitive interest rate on Managed Account cash holdings.

Individually Managed Accounts (IMA)

Praemium’s IMA enables advice businesses to construct portfolios that are more customised to their clients' specific needs and preferences from a broad universe of SMAs and single investment assets. The Praemium IMA experience includes:

  • Superannuation/pension, SMSF and investment and savings solutions.
  • A broad range of single investment assets, listed and unlisted, domestic and international. Supported by a CGT 'what-if' engine for assessing the impact of potential portfolio changes.
  • Access to > 350 professionally managed model portfolios (domestic and international) and 80 Investment Managers with a world class rebalancing engine to ensure investors are always in-line with the chosen strategy.
  • Digital, 24/7 adviser and investor reporting of investment holdings, corporate actions, performance and tax positions.

Made to measure Managed Accounts.

Accessed via the Praemium SMA and Super SMA PDS, the IMA offers broad investment choice, including domestic and international listed securities, exchange-traded products (ETPs), XTBs, hybrids, unlisted managed funds and cash as single investment assets as well as professionally managed SMA model portfolios. 

This choice is complemented by a low minimum investment requirement, competitive admin fees and low brokerage (with aggregated trading and Praemium’s platform scale) to provide value for money for clients. Great and small. With digital acceptance of applications and in-specie transfer of assets, it’s never been easier to establish a managed account with the inherent benefits of an SMA and the additional customisation of an IMA.

Your clients can also benefit from:

  • Beneficial ownership of investments and individual tax positions.
  • Customisations via substitutions, exclusions or locking of investment holdings.
  • Market-leading corporate actions management and tax reporting.
  • A market-leading digital Investor Portal using any wifi or web-enabled device.
  • A very competitive interest rate on Managed Account cash holdings.
  • Value for money at all account sizes.

Virtual Managed Accounts (VMA)

VMAs are investment and SMSF portfolios, managed and administered for clients by your business using Praemium’s non-custodial (Virtual) Managed Accounts solution. The Praemium VMA includes:

  • Market-leading, complex corporate actions and tax reporting and performance benchmarking supported by an unmatched range of market indices.
  • Broad investment asset coverage including all ASX-listed securities, more than 4000 international securities on 40 exchanges and many types of unlisted investments, bonds, managed funds and CMAs, with your choice of execution and custody.
  • High-quality client and business reporting tools, including Investor Portal, Report Publisher and Export Centre.
  • The broadest range of investment data feeds in the market with other assets maintenance functions (e.g. property and collectibles).
  • Praemium’s VMA Administration and SMSF Services enable you to move to a fully outsourced business model.

The non-custodial Managed Accounts solution.

Praemium's proprietary technology is the market leader in complex corporate actions management, performance analytics, asset allocation, tax and multi-asset investment reporting. Investments can include domestic and international listed securities, Exchange Traded Products (ETPs), unlisted managed funds, property, term deposits and CMAs which are transacted externally with your choice of execution and brought together by the most comprehensive range of data feeds to the (VMA) managed accounts platform.

This document does not constitute personal financial advice and is not an offer to invest in any Financial Product offered by, or on behalf of, Praemium.

Praemium VMA also:

  • Enables easy transition of investment portfolios with low client impact and paperwork.
  • Supports data from custody and non-custody services with own execution.
  • Provides access to more than 32 standard client reports and 230 customisation options.
  • Offers administrator access levels for dealer or institutional oversight.
  • Offers optional outsourced administration to Praemium.
  • Has a competitive pricing structure with client billing tools.
  • Complements other in-house broking and execution services & systems with sophisticated APIs, external software data feeds and branding options.
  • Forms part of Praemium's integrated managed accounts platform covering non-custody (VMA) and other custodial options (SMAs, IMAs, UMAs) enabling great flexibility to offer a differentiated service proposition across all client segments, if required.

Unified Managed Accounts (UMA)

Bringing together the benefits of the SMA, IMA and VMA solutions, the Praemium UMA provides the ultimate in transparency and account consolidation. With the Praemium UMA advice businesses and their clients can access:

  • One consolidated reporting view across custodial (SMA/IMA) and non-custodial (VMA) investment assets.
  • An ideal solution for SMSFs covering listed and unlisted, domestic and international, traditional and exotic, professionally managed or directly held investments.
  • Market-leading, complex corporate actions and tax reporting and performance benchmarking supported by an unmatched range of market indices.
  • Additional efficiencies with the option to outsource non-custodial SMSF and VMA administration back to Praemium.

The ultimate Managed Accounts view.

The Praemium UMA with its unique consolidated view of all investment assets, custodial and non-custodial, is the ultimate wealth management solution. It combines all the benefits of the SMA, IMA and VMA into one unified reporting view. Other specific benefits of the UMA include:

  • Investment portfolios tailored by you to the unique needs of your clients with easy transition of existing to the platform.
  • One single, consolidated view across their whole portfolio of investments.
  • A truly innovative digital Investor Portal available via wifi or internet enabled device.
  • Market-leading reporting and data management tools (Investor Portal, Report Publisher, Export Centre) for customisable and bespoke business reporting. 
  • One integrated managed accounts platform covering custodial and non-custodial options (SMA or IMA + VMA = UMA) provides great flexibility to offer a differentiated service proposition across all client segments.
