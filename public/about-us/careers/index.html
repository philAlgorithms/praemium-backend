Working at Praemium

Each of our offices has a benefits package unique to their country. Some practices and benefits include: 

Flexible & Hybrid working

We recognise that flexible work arrangements assist you in balancing the changing demands of work, personal commitments and family responsibilities while continuing to contribute to the business. We offer:

  • Part-time work
  • Job-sharing
  • Flexi-time
  • Paid parental leave
  • Working from home
  • Purchase of additional leave
  • Paid community service leave

Learning & Development

We commit to providing our Team Members with exceptional learning experiences via a blended learning approach with opportunities to cross-skill and continuously develop their capabilities. We offer:

  • Great Start induction program
  • Leadership development program
  • Fortnightly lunch & learn
  • 1-on-1 Mentoring and coaching
  • Ongoing feedback and assessment
  • External courses and industry events

Reward & Recognition

We commit to fostering a culture of high performance and collaboration through, recognising and rewarding efforts, and showing Team Members greatest achievements. We offer:

  • Peer to peer, team and individual recognition
  • High achievement award
  • Refer a friend gift
  • Social and life event celebration

Life at Praemium

It's not all work, work, work. A fun environment is a big part of working at Praemium.

PPS Express Day – global hackathon

It happens in a 24-hour time period and ignites creativity, collaboration and innovation with the simple goal of improving our client, products or employee's experiences.  PPS Express is our in-house Hackathon - a fun competition to see who will achieve glory with their idea. Teams from all our global office have a chance to work on a project of their choice. The aim is to deliver functionality or service that improves the customer experience or improve an internal process that make Praemium more efficient. Anyone can submit ideas and pitch them to the business. Employees can then elect to support that idea by creating a team to turn the idea into reality and have a chance at winning the grand prize and trophy.  It's not just the winning idea, often the ideas that our Hackathon generates become reality. 

 

Corporate games

Each year many of our team members are actively looking to do more together. One of the ways we help achieve this is through our corporate games. These activities arrange from our athletic style events e.g. tennis, golf, etc. All the way through to challenging mind games e.g. chess, poker, etc.

 

 

 

Kids to work 

We offer the opportunity for parents to bring their children to work for a day. Throughout this day team members rotate each hour to interact with the kids and play games with them as a small break from the usual weekly tasks. It’s a great way to show your child around the workplace and give context to where you go each week as well as for them to meet some of your fellow team members.

 

Team building activities and more!

At Praemium we also offer sponsorship to participate in sporting challenges, races and fundraiser events. Every Friday we have drinks, nibbles and a quiz as well as holiday season company parties.
