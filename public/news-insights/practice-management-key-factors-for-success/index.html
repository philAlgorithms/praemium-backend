News & Insights Practice Management - Key Factors for Success

22/07/2021 Podcasts 25 minutes to read Back to all News & Insights

Praemium's Head of Distribution, Martin Morris talks to the founder of advice consultancy Business Health, Terry Bell. They discuss some of the key challenges impacting advice firms today, including profitability, the size and profile of a practice's client base and the impact of COVID on business operating models. With some fascinating insights on what the most successful advice practices are getting right and tips around succession planning, this session will give you plenty of food for thought.

 

Get a copy of the Practice by the Numbers research 

The research outlines how to evaluate your firm on key business performance metrics, giving you useful insights as to how your business compares to the industry average.  With practical insights on how to utilise the findings, you can build a clear picture of how your business is performing and implement ongoing measurement and monitoring designed to keep your business a sustainable and successful one.  


7 key marketplace metrics

Find out how your practice compares to the industry average for these metrics:

  • Revenue per client
  • Clients per adviser
  • Salaries as % of revenue
  • Support staff per adviser
  • Client satisfaction ratings
  • Practice profitability
  • Rent as % of revenue
