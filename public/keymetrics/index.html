Practice by the Numbers 7 key metrics for your advice business

We've teamed up with leading advice consultancy Business Health, to bring you the latest advice industry research 'Practice Management by the Numbers', an analysis of the key financial data from over 300 advice businesses across Australia. 

The research outlines how to evaluate your firm on key business performance metrics, giving you useful insights as to how your business compares to the industry average.  With practical insights on how to utilise the findings, you can build a clear picture of how your business is performing and implement ongoing measurement and monitoring designed to keep your business a sustainable and successful one.  

7 key marketplace metrics

Find out how your practice compares to the industry average for these metrics:

  • Revenue per client
  • Clients per adviser
  • Salaries as % of revenue
  • Support staff per adviser
  • Client satisfaction ratings
  • Practice profitability
  • Rent as % of revenue
